Frida Snilstveit Hoem

Postdoctoral Researcher

University of Bergen | Bjerknes Centre for Climate Research

As a Marie Curie "Shaping European Research Leaders for Marine Sustainability (SEAS)" research fellow at Bjerknes and UiB, I currently research the Atlantic Meridional Overturning Circulation (AMOC) stability during the warm world analogue, The Pliocene.

My work focuses on studying benthic foraminifera (stable isotope) in Pliocene marine sediment deposits to reconstruct Southern Ocean and North Atlantic structure, nutrient and oxygen availability, and primary productivity. I compare this with available multiproxy data and model simulations to elucidate the interactions between climate, oceanographic, and biological processes.
